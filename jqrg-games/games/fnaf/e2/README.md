## 🧠 About the Game

**Five Nights At Epstein's 2** is a survival horror experience where you must endure multiple nights while being hunted by mysterious and unsettling characters. Use strategy, timing, and awareness to survive until morning.

The game combines:

* 🎥 Security camera monitoring
* 😱 Jump scares and suspense
* 🌙 Increasing difficulty each night

---

## 🎯 Objective

Your goal is simple:

> Survive all five nights… if you can.

Watch the cameras, manage your resources wisely, and react quickly to threats. Each night becomes progressively harder, testing your reflexes and decision-making.

---

## 🕹️ Controls

| Action         | Description                          |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| Mouse Click    | Interact with buttons & Flashlight   |
| Right click    | Mask on/off                          |
| Move mouse     | Move your mouse around to look around and open control-panels or camera's |

Then open `index.html` in your browser.
